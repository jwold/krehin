A short post can make one observation and stop. A longer article earns its length by developing an argument, testing it, and leaving the reader somewhere new.

### The useful distinction

Length is not the format. Intent is. This timeline should be comfortable with a sentence, several paragraphs, or a substantial essay without making any of them look misplaced.
