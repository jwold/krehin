What if I don’t have 750 words in me? Years ago I gave myself a challenge, to write 1,000 words a day, six days a week. I did that, more or less, for a few years. It was a wonderful experience, and gave me the necessary muscles to be able to put out words and challenge myself. 

But some days I didn’t have it in me, so I would just journal; trashing words together like an ape on a keyboard. 

Something I’ve often wondered, in the time since, is what I should do if I don’t have the minimum amount of words in me on a given day. Some days I combat that by getting it out. But lately, many days that’s meant not writing at all. I don’t love that. But I also don’t want to put out rubbish to people who pay attention with their time.

Enter this blog. I have lots of shorter thoughts I want to share on a daily basis; but many aren't processed enough to fit into a blog post, or worthy enough of an email blast. That's what this is for. It's my own replacement to Twitter of old. It will be an endless stream of consciousness, hopefully just a little better quality than my journal entries of old.
